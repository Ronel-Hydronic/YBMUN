About Us

YBMUN (Yuva Bharathi MUN) is an educational simulation where students act as diplomats to debate and discuss pressing global issues. Hosted by Yuvabharathi Public School, Coimbatore, YBMUN fosters leadership and diplomacy in the leaders of tomorrow.

Committees

  • United Nations Security Council (UNSC)
  • World Health Organization (WHO)
  • United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)
  • International Court of Justice (ICJ)

Conference Details

The YBMUN 2024 Conference will take place on October 19-20, 2024 at Yuvabharathi Public School, Coimbatore. Join us for an enriching experience where you'll take part in simulations of real-world diplomacy.
