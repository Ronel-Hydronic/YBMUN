About YBMUN

YBMUN (Yuva Bharathi MUN) is an educational simulation where students take on roles of diplomats, engage in debates, and discuss global issues. Hosted by Yuvabharathi Public School, this prestigious event encourages the next generation to understand international relations and diplomacy.

Committees

  • UN Security Council (UNSC)
  • World Health Organization (WHO)
  • United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)
  • International Court of Justice (ICJ)
