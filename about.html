About Us

YBMUN (Yuva Bharathi MUN) is an educational simulation where students act as diplomats to debate and discuss pressing global issues. Hosted by Yuvabharathi Public School, Coimbatore, YBMUN fosters leadership and diplomacy in the leaders of tomorrow.
